Maintaining Sushi

The first step to mainting your sushi is controlling the moisture of the rice.
The simple way I found to keep the sushi soft and moist is by soaking a wet
paper towel and folding it up to fit over the top of all your sushi. By doing
this, it will give the rice extra moisture. after the paper towel is placed
ontop, grab any airtight container and store it in the refridgerator.
