Storing
1. Put the Sushi Rolls/ Sushi Rice in a sealing container of your choice
2. Get a big enough Papertowel piece that can can cover the whole top of the container, but fit under the lid
3. Soak the paper towel in the sink until its dripping
4. fold the papertowel once and drain until not dripping, but still clearly wet
5. unfold the papertowel and put ontop of the sushi roll or Sushi rice
6. close the container and store in refrigerator

What you'll Need
☻1x Paper Towel
☻A Sink
☻A container

Prepping
The only thing you'll need to do to prepare The Rice or Sushi is to simply heat the container up with the lid off for about 20-30 Seconds with the Wet Papertowel on the Food
