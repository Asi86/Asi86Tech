Mac n Cheese Recipe
Pasta Ingredients:

  • 4 Cups Water
  • 1 Teaspoon Sea Salt
  • 2 1/2 Cups Pasta
Cheese Mix Ingredients:
  • 2 Tablespoon Butter
  • 2 Tablespoon Flour
  • 1 Cup Low Sodium Chicken Broth

  • 1 1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 1/2 Cup Jack Cheese Shredded
       (Alternatively use 1 1/4 Cup Shredded Jack
        Along with 1 1/4 Cup Sharp Cheddar)
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • Optional: Add Breadcrumbs, Bacon Bits, or other ingredients
Instructions:
  1. Pour all the Pasta ingredients into a pot, cook till pasta is squishy.
  2. Drain Pasta And put it into an 8in x 8in Pan
  3. Preheat oven to 350F
  4. Mix the First 3 ingredients into a pot and whisk until completly liquid
  5. Turn stove on High and Cook pot for 2-3 Minutes
  6. turn The burner off and Move pot off of the hot burner
  7. Add Cheese, Heavy Cream, And Black Pepper to the pot, stir for 10seconds
  8. Pour Pasta into pot and stir till cheese mixed with pasta
  9. Pour the contents of the pot into the 8in x 8in Pan and put into the oven @350F
  10. Cook for 10min and Enjoy
