Pasta Ingredients:
  • 4 Cups Water
  • 1 Teaspoon Sea Salt
  • 2 1/2 Cups Pasta
Cheese Mix Ingredients:
  • 2 Tablespoon Butter
  • 2 Tablespoon Flour
  • 1 Cup Low Sodium Chicken Broth

  • 1 1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 1/2 Cup Jack Cheese Shredded
       (Alternatively use 1 1/4 Cup Shredded Jack
        Along with 1 1/4 Cup Sharp Cheddar)
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • Optional: Add Breadcrumbs, Bacon Bits, or other ingredients
Instructions:
  1. Pour all the Pasta ingredients into a pot, cook till pasta is squishy.
  2. Drain Pasta And put the pasta into an 8in x 8in Pan.
  3. Preheat oven to 350F.
  4. Wash the pot and reuse for the next step.
  5. Melt 2 TableSpoons butter in microwave and pour into your pot.
  6. put the flour and chicken broth in the pot with the melted butter.
  7. Whisk the 3 ingredients inside the pot until completly liquid.
  8. Move the pot onto a stove and turn it on High.
  9. Add Cheese, Heavy Cream, And Black Pepper to the pot.
  10. Stir pot innards for 10 seconds or until cheese melts.
  11. Turn The burner off and Move pot off of the hot burner.
  12. Pour cooked Pasta into pot and stir utill mixed.
  13. Pour the contents of the pot into the 8in x 8in Pan.
  14. Place your pan inside the oven @350F, Cook for 10min and Enjoy!!

Dry Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 TableSpoon White Sugar
  • 3 1/2 TeaSpoon Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Sea Salt(iodised works fine though)
Liquid Ingredients:
  • 1 1/4 cup Dairy Milk
  • 3 TableSpoons Salted Butter
  • 1 Egg(no shell)
  • (optional)Chocolate chips, mints, blueberries, etc.
Instructions:
  1. Grab a big Bowl(has to fit a whole bag o popcorn).
  2. put in all the Dry ingredients into the big bowl.
  3. Melt the Salted Butter into a bowl and pour it into the big bowl.
  4. Pour in the Milk and egg into the big bowl and whisk till liquid.
  5. If Applicable, Put in your optional ingredients in desired quantity.
  6. Bring out your griddle and set to 350F(or if your impatient 450F works)
  7. Cover the bottom of the Griddle in butter
  8. Pour your Pancake batter until about 4-6in wide.
  9. Flip Pancake over once the centre has bubbles.
  10. Cook the Pancake for the same time it took to reveal bubbles.
  11. Repeat steps 7-10 with remaining Pancake Batter and eat.

Dashi Ingredients:
  • 2L (2 Quarts) Water
  • 30g (1 Ounce) Dried Kombu
  • 30g (1 Ounce) Bonito Flakes

Toasted Spices:
  • 7g (2 tablespoons) Whole Coriander
  • 6g (1 tablespoon) Whole Cumin Seed
  • 6g (1 tablespoon) Mustard Seed
  • 6g (2 1/2 teaspoons) Cardamom
  • 5g (2 teaspoons) Black Peppercorns
  • 2g (1/2 teaspoon) Fennel Seed

Spices:
  • 1 or 2 strips (1g) dehydrated orange peel
  • 1 (2-inch) piece cinnamon (3g)
  • 3 cloves
  • 1/2 star anise pod

Fine Spices:
  • 16g (2 Tablespoons) Ground Turmeric
  • 1 Pinch of Grated Nutmeg
  • 1g (1/4 Teaspoon) Chilli Powder
    can go up to 2g (1/2 Teaspoon)
Stew Ingredients:
  • 1 pound (450g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) vegetable oil, plus more as needed
  • 1 large yellow onion (1 pound; 450g), diced
  • 8 ounces carrots (225g; about 3 medium), peeled and cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 quart (950ml) homemade chicken stock or store-bought low-sodium broth
  • 950ml (1 Quart) of Dashi
  • 1 pound (450g) potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 (6-ounce) apple, peeled, cored, and finely grated, minced, or puréed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (4 ounces; 110g)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour (2 ounces; 55g)
  • 1 (2-inch) piece peeled fresh ginger, finely grated
  • 3 tablespoons (25g) Curry Spice Blend
  • 1 cup frozen peas (5.5 ounces; 160g)
  • 2 Cups Uncooked Rice(4 cups cooked)
Instructions:
  1. Take the spices and cook them in a dry skillet for about 5 minutes, or until they smell.
  2. in the meantime, get the orange peel and microwave for about 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  3. grind the orange and toasted spices in a spice grinder until very fine.
  4. add the Remaining Spices to the grinder until everything is fine.
  5. Store Grinded Spices into Container(we will only need 25g (3 Tablespoons) later)
  6. Put the First Two Dashi Ingredients in a saucepan and cook on Medium Heat until Simmering.
  7. Turn heat off and place Bonito Flakes into the pot, let sit for 5 minutes.
  8. Pour into Bowl while filtering out the Bonito Flakes and Kombu.

For the Curry Spice Blend: In a spice grinder, combine coriander, cumin, fenugreek, cardamom, black peppercorns, fennel, cinnamon, cloves, star anise, and orange peel (if using) and grind to a fine powder. Empty into a small bowl and combine with turmeric, chili powder, and nutmeg, then set aside.
  14. For the Stew: Season chicken all over with salt and pepper. In a large Dutch oven, heat oil over medium-high heat until shimmering. Add chicken and cook, turning, until browned on both sides, about 6 minutes. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside..
  15. Melt the Salted Butter into a bowl and pour it into the big bowl.
  16. Add onion to Dutch oven, lower heat to medium-low, and cook, stirring, until golden, about 10 minutes. Add carrots and cook for 4 minutes. Add chicken stock and dashi and bring to a simmer over high heat; reduce heat to maintain a simmer.
  17. Cut up chicken into bite-size pieces and add back to pot, along with any accumulated juices. Add potatoes and apple and cook at a gentle simmer until potatoes are tender and carrots can easily be pierced with a fork, about 15 minutes.
  18. Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat until foaming. Stir in flour and cook, stirring, until roux is a deep caramel brown, about 20 minutes. Stir in ginger and 3 tablespoons (25g) Curry Spice Blend and cook for 1 minute.
  19. Scrape roux into stew pot, stir well, and simmer until broth has thickened. Season with salt and pepper. Stir in peas, if using, and cook until heated through.
  20. Serve curry with cooked rice and pickled ginger and/or rakkyo.
