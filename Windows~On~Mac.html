Windows on Mac

  • NOTE: you need an empty 16gb or bigger usb AND atleast 25gb on your Mac
  1. Search "Windows 10 Download" in any search bar.
  2. Click on the result that has www.microsoft.com as the web address/URL.
  3. Click "select edition".
  4. Select the most up to date option.
  5. Click "Confirm".
  6. Scroll down to the "Select the product language".
  7. Click on the drop down menu and select your main language.
  8. Click the "confirm" Button.
  9. Once the webpage loads, click on the "64-BIT Download" button.
  10. When you have the file, go to the top right corner of the screen.
  11. Click the magnifying glass and type "Disk Utility".
  12. Click on the empty 16gb Drive.(Click for Image)
  13. Click on the Restore Button.
  14. Click the "Image..." button above the "Restore" Button.
  15. Navigate to the "Windows.iso" file from earlier.
  16. Click the "Choose" Button.
  17. Click the "Restore" Button.
