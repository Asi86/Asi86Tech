Enable Repeating Keys

  1. Press the command key and the space bar.
  2. Type in "Terminal"
  3. Press return after a black box icon with a <_ is inside it.
  4. Wait 10 seconds.(or until a black window shows up)
  5. Click in the black window (this should enable you to type)
  6. Copy and Paste "defaults write -g ApplePressAndHoldEnabled -bool false" into the window

How to transfer messages from the Messages app(AKA "iChat") from one Mac to another

  1. on the old mac, press the command key and the space bar.
  2. Type in "Terminal"
  3. Press return after a black box icon with a <_ is inside it.
  4. Wait 10 seconds.(or until a black window shows up)
  5. Click in the black window (this should enable you to type)
  6. Copy and Paste "open ~/Library/Messages" into the window
  7. Copy everything in that folder to a USB.
  8. Do steps 1-5 on the newer Mac.
  9. Eject the usb from the old mac and plug it into the new mac
  10. Copy the files from the USB you put there in step 6.
  11. Paste the files in the window you opened in step 5 on the new mac
  12. Right-Click where there isnt an item(you may have to go to the top of finder and click the icon to the left of the four lines)
  13. Left-Click "Get Info"
  14. Click the Arrow facing right next to the words "Sharing & Permission"
  15. Click The Lock in the bottom right corner of the window
  16. Enter an adminstrator Username and Password
  17. Click any "Read Only"
  18. Click "Read & Write" from the dropdown menu"
  19. Repeat Steps 17 and 18 if there are any "Read Only" Options Left
  20. Click on the gear at the bottom of the window
  21. Click "Apply to all enclosed items..."
  22. Click the "OK" Button in blue
  23. Thats All Folks!!!

  4. click the icon with the "recovery" text below it
