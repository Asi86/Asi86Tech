Enable Repeating Keys

  1. Press the command key and the space bar.
  2. Type in "Terminal"
  3. Press return after a black box icon with a <_ is inside it.
  4. Wait 10 seconds.(or until a black window shows up)
  5. Click in the black window (this should enable you to type)
  6. Copy and Paste "defaults write -g ApplePressAndHoldEnabled -bool false" into the window
  7. Press Enter

How to transfer messages from the Messages app(AKA "iChat") from one Mac to another

  1. on the old mac, press the command key and the space bar.
  2. Type in "Terminal"
  3. Press return after a black box icon with a <_ is inside it.
  4. Wait 10 seconds.(or until a black window shows up)
  5. Click in the black window (this should enable you to type)
  6. Copy and Paste "open ~/Library/Messages" into the window
  7. Press Enter
  8. Copy everything in that folder to a USB.
  9. Do steps 1-6 on the newer Mac.
  10. Eject the usb from the old mac and plug it into the new mac
  11. Copy the files from the USB you put there in step 6.
  12. Paste the files in the window you opened in step 5 on the new mac
  13. Right-Click where there isnt an item(you may have to go to the top of finder and click the icon to the left of the four lines)
  14. Left-Click "Get Info"
  15. Click the Arrow facing right next to the words "Sharing & Permission"
  16. Click The Lock in the bottom right corner of the window
  17. Enter an adminstrator Username and Password
  18. Click any "Read Only"
  19. Click "Read & Write" from the dropdown menu"
  20. Repeat Steps 17 and 18 if there are any "Read Only" Options Left
  21. Click on the gear at the bottom of the window
  22. Click "Apply to all enclosed items..."
  23. Click the "OK" Button in blue
  24. Thats All Folks!!!

How to reset Your Password(if you didnt enable Disk Encryption

  1. Make Sure Your Mac Is Powered Down
  2. Hold the left Option Key and press the power button
  3. you should see 2 icons(usually)
  4. click the icon with the "recovery" text below it
  5. Press the Return Key
  6. Once it boots, you should see 4 options and a bar at the top
  7. go to the grey bar at the top and click on the utilities dropdown menu
  8. from there, select the terminal app
  9. Once it opens, click inside the window
  10. Type in "resetpassword", no spaces or caps
  11. click on the window that pops up
  12. select the account that you would like to reset
  13. enter the new password and dont forget to click apply
  14. restart the computer once the "password has been changed" window pops up
  15. type in the new password and go to system preferences
  16. sign out of icloud and restart

Enable opening files downloaded from unauthorized sources

  1. Press the command key and the space bar.
  2. Type in "Terminal"
  3. Press return after a black box icon with a <_ is inside it.
  4. Wait 10 seconds.(or until a black window shows up)
  5. Click in the black window (this should enable you to type)
  6. Copy and Paste "sudo spctl --master-disable" into the window
  7. Press Enter
  8. Type In Your password. your password will be invisible.

Labeling USB Boot Drives

  1. Press the command key and the space bar.
  2. Type in "Terminal"
  3. Press return after a black box icon with a <_ is inside it.
  4. Wait 10 seconds.(or until a black window shows up)
  5. Click in the black window (this should enable you to type)
  6. Copy and Paste "sudo bless --folder -label " into the window
  7. Press Enter
  8. FOR EXAMPLE
    sudo bless --folder "/Volumes/Mac OS X Install" -label "Mojave For Oldies"

Erasing Storage Drives

  1. Press the command key and the space bar.
  2. Type in "Terminal"
  3. Press return after a black box icon with a <_ is inside it.
  4. Wait 10 seconds.(or until a black window shows up)
  5. Click in the black window (this should enable you to type)
  6. Copy and Paste "diskutil eraseDisk Format DriveName /dev/disk#s#" into the window
  7. Press Enter
  8. FOR EXAMPLE
    diskutil eraseDisk JHFS+ Untitled /dev/disk2
    or
    diskutil eraseDisk JHFS+ Untitled /dev/disk2s1
